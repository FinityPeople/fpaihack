## What you'll need

At minimum:

- A laptop (Mac or Windows)
- A browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox)
- Your corporate credentials (for Databricks access)
- An Anthropic account and/or OpenAI/ChatGPT account (for the AI tools)
